# Bogotá

**Archetype:** Split personality — Sunday = Copenhagen, Monday = SF-worse
**Mode share:** ~5% weekday
**Most surprising finding:** The Ciclovía/CicloRuta safety gap is *quantified*: a PMC study found 51% of Ciclovía users feel safe from traffic vs. far fewer on CicloRutas, and a cyclist with a flat tire gets help 73% of the time on Sunday vs. 28% on weekdays. Families aren't irrational for treating them as two different cities.

## Per-segment quality — Wildly variable

The 540–600 km CicloRuta system ranges from well-built separated tracks (Carrera 7 new corridor, Calle 13) to sidewalk-shared paths that are "obstacle courses" of pedestrians, vendors, and driveways. Many crossings mix with car traffic unsafely.

## Network continuity — Extensive on paper, fragmented in practice

Segments exist in most localidades but quality breaks at every major crossing.

## What "protected" means locally

"Cicloruta" means a designated space, not necessarily a protected one. Often it's a sidewalk stripe. Newer Carrera 7 and Calle 13 builds are closer to true separation.

## Beloved family routes

- **Sunday Ciclovía** — 120 km of streets closed 7am–2pm Sundays, the only time most Bogotá parents report feeling safe with kids
- **Parque El Virrey → Carrera 7 corridor** (Chapinero, green, connected)
- **Parkway in La Soledad**

## Avoided corridors on weekdays

- **Chapinero Central, La Capuchina, Ricaurte, Santa Bárbara Occidental, Paloquemao** — top theft localities per Concejo de Bogotá 2025 report
- Night ciclorutas are flagged for robbery, not just traffic

## Vocabulary

*ciclorruta* (permanent), *ciclovía* (Sunday open-streets), *bicicorredor, Rutas Seguras, Bogotá en Bici* (school program, ages 4+)

## Routing-model implications

- **Time-of-week edges are essential** — Sunday Ciclovía corridors become LTS-1 only on Sundays
- **Theft is a routing factor** — treat as an extra dimension, possibly a per-localidad penalty
- The Bogotá en Bici school program starts independent riding at age 18 — the city itself doesn't claim kids can use the CicloRuta alone
