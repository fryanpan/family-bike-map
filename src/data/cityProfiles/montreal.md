Prefer the REV (Réseau Express Vélo) axes — Saint-Denis, Peel, Bellechasse, Viger — and the Lachine Canal path. In winter, only trust routes that are plowed; a plowed REV beats an unplowed painted lane, and an unplowed lane is effectively removed. Avoid non-REV arterials outside the Plateau (Papineau, Sherbrooke stretches).
