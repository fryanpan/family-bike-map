Default routing works on almost every street. The low-stress fietspad network is genuinely citywide, fietsstraten are the gold standard, and mixed traffic on residential streets is already calmed. The real routing concern here is bike congestion and unpredictable tourists and scooters on the busiest paths (Damrak, Rokin) — prefer calmer parallel routes.
