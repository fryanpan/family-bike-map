Prefer Fahrradstraßen and fully separated bike paths. Don't trust painted bike lanes — they sit in the door zone here. Cobblestones and sett paving are rough on kid bikes, especially when wet; route around the old town. The Mauerweg is the gold standard for family rides. Quiet residential streets are acceptable detours; network gaps are real.
