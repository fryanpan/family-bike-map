Prefer the riverside bikeways along Tamsui, Keelung, Xindian, and Jingmei — they're fully car-free behind the flood wall. Floodgates are mandatory access points; plan routes around them and prefer the river trunk plus the shortest possible on-street tail. On-street infrastructure is hostile (bikes share the scooter lane) — minimize street distance.
