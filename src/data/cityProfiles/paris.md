Prefer the REV axes (Rivoli, Sébastopol) and any street in a 30 km/h zone (almost all of them now). Rue aux écoles are car-free during school hours — prefer them then. Avoid Étoile, Bastille, and Nation roundabouts and any Périphérique-adjacent boulevard. Canal Saint-Martin and Canal de l'Ourcq towpaths are the best family routes.
