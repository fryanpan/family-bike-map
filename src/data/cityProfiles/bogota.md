On Sundays 7am–2pm, the Ciclovía is gold — 120 km of streets closed to cars, prefer it heavily then. Weekdays, trust the newer Carrera 7 and Calle 13 builds; otherwise treat the CicloRuta network as unreliable (many segments are sidewalk stripes). Avoid high-theft zones — Chapinero Central, La Capuchina, Ricaurte, Paloquemao, Santa Bárbara Occidental.
