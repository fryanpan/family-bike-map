Separation barely exists — the model is slow everything down, not separate everyone. Trust narrow residential streets in Zone 30 areas and sidewalks where cycling is legally permitted. Avoid Kanpachi (Ring Road 8), Yamate-dori, Meiji-dori, and other arterials entirely. Assume an e-assist mamachari — hills are load-bearing. Kids under 13 can legally ride on sidewalks.
