Inside a superilla is safe — treat superblock interiors as low-stress. Prefer carril bici on Consell de Cent, Diagonal, and the Passeig Marítim seafront. Avoid Gran Via de les Corts Catalanes, Meridiana, and Via Laietana. Crossing between superblocks on arterials is the weak link — prefer routes that stay inside one superilla where possible.
