Prefer Reforma, Chapultepec, and Condesa and Roma side streets. On Sundays Muévete en Bici opens ~55 km of car-free streets — prefer those heavily on Sundays. Don't trust the Insurgentes or Tlalpan ciclovías mid-week — they're taxi-invaded despite the bollards. Skip outdoor riding on Contingencia Ambiental days.
