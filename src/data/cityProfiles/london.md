Prefer segregated Cycleways (C3, C4) and traffic-free paths (Regent's Canal, Thames Path, Richmond Park). Don't trust legacy Quietway labels — many are just signs on rat-runs. Waltham Forest is the one borough with a genuinely connected low-stress network. Avoid CS7 Tooting and the old CS2 Whitechapel stretches.
