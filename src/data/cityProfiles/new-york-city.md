Prefer the greenways — Hudson River, East River, Prospect Park loop, Central Park. Parking-protected paint lanes are shaky; trust them only where there's a real curb. Avoid Queens Boulevard, Manhattan crosstown paint lanes, and outer-borough arterials. Most genuinely family-usable routes are park-internal, not connected across the city.
