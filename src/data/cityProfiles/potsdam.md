Like Berlin but quieter. Park spines — Sanssouci, Neuer Garten, Havel, Mauerweg — act as the family network; prefer them strongly. Altstadt and Holländisches Viertel cobblestones are the main hazard; route around them. Villa-district side streets are genuinely calm; low-traffic residential is acceptable. No plastic-post "protected" lanes — if it's separated it's a real curb or a Fahrradstraße.
