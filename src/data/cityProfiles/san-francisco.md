Only trust fully car-free paths — JFK Promenade, Panhandle, Sunset Dunes — and well-respected Slow Streets like Page and Lake. Plastic-post "protected" lanes like Valencia are NOT safe for kids; treat them as unprotected. Cap continuous climbs around 6% and assume an e-bike for anything hillier. Accept longer detours via the Wiggle to avoid hills.
